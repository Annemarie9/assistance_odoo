Website themes
==============

Odoo offers numerous themes to shape your website\'s style, including
its colors, fonts, and layouts. When setting up your site using the Odoo
website configurator, you are prompted to select a theme that aligns
with your desired aesthetic. If you wish to modify the theme options
later, navigate to the website builder by clicking the
`Edit`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button and access the
`Theme`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab. The following sections
are available:

-   `Colors`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: The website builder
    relies on palettes composed of five colors: two
    `Main`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} colors and three
    `Light & Dark`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} colors.

    You can also customize the `Color Presets`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"} that have been defined automatically by the website
    builder according to the five colors defined above. Click the arrow
    next to a color preset to update it. Each color preset contains
    colors for your building block\'s `Background`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"}, `Text`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Headings`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Links`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Primary Buttons`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}, and
    `Secondary Buttons`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

    

    **To apply a color preset** to a building block of your site, select
    it, go to the `Customize`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab,
    click the `Background`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button,
    and select the preset.

    ::: {.note}
    ::: {.title}
    Note
    :::

    Changing a color preset automatically updates the colors of both the
    default preset and the building blocks where the preset is used.
    :::

-   `Website`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: From this section, you
    can `Switch Theme`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Add a Language <../configuration/translate>`{.interpreted-text
    role="doc"}, select the `Page Layout`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"}, and customize the `Background`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"} by uploading your own image.

-   `Paragraph`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: Customize the
    formatting of your website\'s paragraph [\<p\>]{.title-ref}
    elements.

    ::: {.tip}
    ::: {.title}
    Tip
    :::

    The `Font Family`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} field contains
    fonts that are hosted and served by Google servers. To add another
    font, click `Add a Google Font`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    and, in the popup window, click `fonts.google.com`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"}.

    

    Select a font you like, copy the address of the page, paste it in
    the `Google Font
    address`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} field, then click
    `Save and Reload`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}. The new font
    applies to your entire website.
    :::

-   `Headings`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: Customize the
    formatting of your headings.

-   `Button`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: Customize the
    `Primary Style`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
    `Secondary Style`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} buttons.

    

-   `Link`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: To edit the style of the
    hyperlinks available on your website.

-   `Input Fields`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: Customize fields
    where you can enter data, e.g., a search bar or a form.

-   `Advanced`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: You can hide the
    header bar of your website using the `Show Header`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"} button, inject
    `head and body code <analytics/google-tag-manager>`{.interpreted-text
    role="ref"}, enter your `Google Map Custom Key`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"}, change the colors of the
    `Success`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Info`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
    `Warning`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}, and
    `Error`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} pop up messages by
    clicking the related `Status Colors`{.interpreted-text
    role="guilabel"} buttons, and customize the
    `Grays`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} elements of your site.
