Inner content
=============

The `Inner content`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
`building blocks <../building_blocks>`{.interpreted-text role="doc"}
allow you to add elements such as videos, images, and
`social media buttons <inner_content/social_media>`{.interpreted-text
role="ref"}, into pre-existing blocks.

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

To add a building block, click `Edit`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"}, select the desired building block under the
`Blocks`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab, and drag and drop it
onto the page. To access its settings, click it and go to the
`Customize`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab, where the available
options depend on the type of block selected.
:::

Social media {#inner_content/social_media}
------------

The `Social Media`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} block inserts
clickable buttons leading to your social network\'s URL. By default, the
buttons display the icons of seven major social networks. You can click
`Add New Social Network`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} to create a
new button and switch the buttons next to a URL to turn them on or off.



::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

You cannot edit the default icons but can edit the ones you added by
clicking `Add New Social Network`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}. To
do so, select the icon, then click the `Replace`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} button found under the `Customize`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} tab\'s `Icon`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
section, and either select one of the available icons or click the
`Images`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab and upload an image or
add its URL.
:::
