Menus
=====

Menus are used to organize your website's content and help visitors
navigate through your web pages effectively. User-friendly and
well-structured website menus also play a crucial role in improving
`search engine rankings <seo>`{.interpreted-text role="doc"}.

Odoo allows you to customize the content and appearance of your
website\'s menu to your needs.

Menu editor
-----------

The menu editor allows you to edit your website\'s menu and add
`regular menu items
<website/regular-menus>`{.interpreted-text role="ref"} and
`mega menus <website/mega-menus>`{.interpreted-text role="ref"}.

To edit your website\'s menu, go to
`Website --> Site --> Menu Editor`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}. From there, you can:

-   **rename** a menu item or change its URL using the
    `Edit Menu Item`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon;
-   **delete** a menu item using the
    `Delete Menu Item`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon;
-   **move** a menu item by dragging and dropping it to the desired
    place in the menu;
-   **create a regular drop-down menu** by dragging and dropping the
    sub-menu items to the right, underneath their parent menu.



::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

You can also access the menu editor by clicking `Edit`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"}, selecting any menu item and clicking the
`Edit Menu`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon.


:::

### Adding regular menu items {#website/regular-menus}

By default, pages are added to the menu as regular menu items when
`they are created
<../pages>`{.interpreted-text role="doc"}. You can also add regular menu
items from the menu editor by clicking `Add
Menu Item`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}. Enter the
`Name`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and URL of the related page in
the pop-up window that appears on the screen and click
`OK`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

::: {.tip}
::: {.title}
Tip
:::

In the `URL or Email`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} field, you can
type [/]{.title-ref} to search for a page on your website or
[\#]{.title-ref} to search for an existing custom anchor.
:::

### Adding mega menus {#website/mega-menus}

Mega menus are similar to drop-down menus, but instead of a simple list
of sub-menus, they display a panel divided into groups of navigation
options. This makes them suitable for websites with large amounts of
content, as they can help include all of your web pages in the menu
while still making all menu items visible at once. Mega menus can also
be structured more visually than regular drop-down menus, for example,
through layout, typography, and icons.



To create a mega menu, go to
`Website --> Site --> Menu Editor`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"} and click `Add Mega Menu Item`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"}. Enter the `Name`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} of
the mega menu in the pop-up, click `OK`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"}, then `Save`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

To adapt the options and layout of the mega menu, click it in the
navigation bar, then click `Edit`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.
Mega menus are composed of building blocks, which means you can
customize each component individually using inline formatting, as well
as the options available in the `Customize`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} tab in the website builder. For example, you can:

-   edit the text directly in the building block;

-   edit a menu item\'s URL by selecting the menu item, then clicking
    the `Edit link`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button in the
    small preview pop-up. Type [/]{.title-ref} to search for a page on
    your website, or [\#]{.title-ref} to search for an existing custom
    anchor.

    

-   move a menu item by dragging and dropping the related block to the
    desired position in the mega menu;

-   delete a menu item by deleting the related block.

::: {.tip}
::: {.title}
Tip
:::

You can adapt the general layout of the mega menu by selecting the
desired `Template`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Size`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} in the
`Mega menu`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} section in the
`Customize`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab in the website
builder.
:::

Header and navigation bar appearance
------------------------------------

To customize the appearance of your website\'s menu, click
`Edit`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}, then select the navigation
bar or any menu item. You can then adapt the fields in the
`Header`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Navbar`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} sections in the
`Customize`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} tab in the website
builder.
