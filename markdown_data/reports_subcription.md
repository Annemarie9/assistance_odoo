Subscription reports
====================

The Odoo **Subscriptions** app provides a series of reporting pages to
help companies analyze how subscriptions are performing.

On the *Subscriptions Analysis* reporting page, users can view specific
data related to recurring subscriptions, quantity of subscriptions,
in-progress or paused subscriptions, and more.

The *Retention Analysis* reporting page provides an organized table of
subscription retention percentages over any period of time.

The *MRR Breakdown* reporting page clearly divides
`MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)`{.interpreted-text role="abbr"} and
`ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)`{.interpreted-text role="abbr"} metrics
for subscriptions into various graphs, lists, and charts.

And the *MRR Analysis* reporting page offers a time-based collection of
analytics showcasing how subscription
`MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)`{.interpreted-text role="abbr"} and
`ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)`{.interpreted-text role="abbr"} have
changed over the course of any given period of time.

Reporting page elements
-----------------------

All subscriptions-based reporting pages can be accessed via the
`Reporting`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} header menu in the
**Subscriptions** app.

The following sections describe elements found on each reporting page.

### Filters and Group By

*Filters* are used to narrow down metrics to show specific analytics,
whereas groupings (via the *Group By* option) are used to gather the
data from specific sections into groups for more organized analysis.

This section refers to both filters and groupings, as a combination of
the two can be saved in the *Favorites* section.

To modify the results being shown on any reporting page, click the
`fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(down arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon to the right of
the search bar. Doing so reveals a drop-down menu of detailed filter and
grouping options.

If desired, a filter or grouping (or combination of filters and/or
groupings) can be saved in the `Favorites`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} section of that drop-down menu. To do so, click the
`fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(down arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon beside
`Save current search`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}, located
beneath the `Favorites`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} section.

This reveals a field to assign a title to the favorite filter. Two
options are also found beneath the title field:
`Default filter`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Shared`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

Ticking the checkbox beside `Default filter`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} makes the newly-favorited filter the default option for
that reporting page.

Ticking the checkbox beside `Shared`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
makes the newly-favorited filter available to other users in the
database.

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

The `Default filter`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Shared`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} options are **not**
required, and only *one* of these options can be selected at a time.
:::

To save the filter, click `Save`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} in
the `Favorites`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} section of the
drop-down filter menu.

When clicked, that saved filter appears beneath the
`Favorites`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} column of the drop-down
filter menu, and a `fa-star`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(gold star)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon appears beside the
favorite filter\'s name in the search bar.

### Views

On the `Subscription Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"},
`MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}, and
`MRR Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting pages, three
different view options are located in the upper-right corner.

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

There are *no* other view options available on the
`Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page.
:::

The available view options, from left to right, are:

-   `Graph`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `List`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Pivot`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

{.align-center}

Each view has its own series of related view-specific visual options.

#### Graph view

With the graph view selected, the following options appear between the
search bar and visual representation of the data. These graph-specific
options are located to the right of the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} and `Insert in Spreadsheet`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} buttons.

{.align-center}

The first three options, from left to right, represent different
graph-related views. The remaining options represent different ways to
organize and visualize that specific graph-related data.

From left to right, the specific graph-related view options are:

-   `fa-bar-chart`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Bar Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in a bar chart format.
-   `fa-line-chart`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Line Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in a line chart format.
-   `fa-pie-chart`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Pie Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in a pie chart format.

Each graph view option has its own series of specific visual options,
which are represented by the available buttons that appear to the right
of the selected graph-related view option.

When the `fa-bar-chart`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`Bar Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} graph view is selected,
the following visual options are available:

-   `fa-database`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Stacked`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data in
    a stacked visual format.
-   `fa-sort-amount-desc`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Descending`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in descending order.
-   `fa-sort-amount-asc`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Ascending`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in ascending order.

When the `Line Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} graph view is
selected, the following visual options are available:

-   `fa-database`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Stacked`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data in
    a stacked visual format.
-   `fa-signal`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Cumulative`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in accumulated, increasing format.
-   `fa-sort-amount-desc`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Descending`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in descending order.
-   `fa-sort-amount-asc`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Ascending`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: showcases the data
    in ascending order.

When the `Pie Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} graph view is
selected, there are no additional visual options.

#### List view

With the list view selected, the subscription metrics being analyzed are
displayed in a simple list, which can be fully customized by using any
of the available filters or groupings in the drop-down filter menu
(accessible via the `fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(down arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon to the right of
the search bar).

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

With list view selected, the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu and `Insert in
Spreadsheet`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button are *not*
available.
:::

#### Pivot view

With the pivot view selected, the subscription metrics are displayed in
a data table, which can be fully customized.

The pivot data table can be customized using the options available in
the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu, and/or
the filter grouping options available in the filter drop-down menu
(accessible via the `fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(down arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon to the right of
the search bar).

Three pivot-specific options are available, located to the right of the
`Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu and
`Insert in Spreadsheet`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button.

{.align-center}

From left to right, those pivot-specific view options are:

-   `fa-exchange`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Flip axis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: the [x]{.title-ref}
    and [y]{.title-ref} axis of the pivot data table flip.
-   `fa-arrows`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Expand all`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: all the available
    rows and columns of the pivot data table expand fully.
-   `fa-download`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
    `Download .xlsx`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}: the pivot data
    table is downloaded as an `.xlsx`{.interpreted-text role="file"}
    file.

### Measures

The graph and pivot reporting pages have their own metric-specific
`Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu of
data-related options to choose from, located in the upper-left corner,
above the visual representation of metrics.

{.align-center}

When the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button is
clicked, a series of selectable measures becomes available, via a
drop-down menu. When any of the options are selected from the
`Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu, the chosen
metrics related to that specific measure appear on the reporting page.

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

For more information on the different measures that can be utilized on
each reporting page, refer to the
`specific reporting page breakdowns <subscriptions/reports/reporting>`{.interpreted-text
role="ref"} found below in this documentation.
:::

### Insert in spreadsheet

Beside the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu,
there is an `Insert in Spreadsheet`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
button.

When clicked, the ability to add the configured data currently being
showcased on the reporting page into a new or pre-existing spreadsheet
or dashboard becomes available, via a pop-up window.

{.align-center}

Select the desired option from this pop-up window, then click
`Confirm`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

Reporting pages {#subscriptions/reports/reporting}
---------------

In the Odoo **Subscriptions** app, there are four different reporting
pages available.

To access, analyze, and customize various reports related to
subscriptions, navigate to `Subscriptions app`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}, and click the `Reporting`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu in the header to reveal the following
reporting pages:

-   `Subscriptions`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Retention`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `MRR Timeline`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

Clicking any of those options reveals a separate, fully-customizable
reporting page focusing on that particular aspect of subscription data.

The following is a breakdown of those four specific reporting pages.

### Subscriptions analysis

To access the `Subscriptions Analysis`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} reporting page, navigate to
`Subscriptions app --> Reporting --> Subscriptions`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}.

By default, the `Bar Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option,
in the `Graph`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} view, is selected on
the `Subscriptions Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
reporting page.

The following filters are also present in the search bar:
`In Progress or Paused`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Recurring`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

{.align-center}

When the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} button on the
`Subscriptions Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} page is
clicked, a series of metric-related options becomes available as a
drop-down menu.

{.align-center}

The metric-related options in the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on the
`Subscriptions Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} page are:

-   `Monthly Recurring`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Quantity`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Recurring Revenue`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Untaxed Total`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Yearly Recurring`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Count`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

The `Monthly Recurring`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} measure
option is selected by default.
:::

When any of those available measures are clicked, Odoo displays that
selected data on the reporting page for further analysis.

### Retention analysis

To access the `Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
reporting page, navigate to
`Subscriptions app --> Reporting --> Retention`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}.

The `Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting
page differs from the other **Subscriptions** app reporting pages, in
that it does **not** provide any additional view options. The data on
this page is only presented in a customizable data chart.

{.align-center}

When the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on
the `Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting
page is clicked, a series of metric-related options become available.

{.align-center}

The metric-related options in the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on the `Retention
Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page are:

-   `Amount to invoice`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Margin`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Margin (%)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Prepayment percentage`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Shipping Weight`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Unpaid Amount`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Count`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

The `Count`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} measure option is
selected by default.
:::

To the right of the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
drop-down menu on the `Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} page is an additional drop-down menu containing
different time periods. The default time period is
`Month`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

When clicked, a drop-down menu of various time period options become
available.

{.align-center}

The time period options are:

-   `Day`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Week`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Month`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Year`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

When a time period option from this drop-down menu is selected, the
`Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page
showcases data for the configured measures and filters within that time
period.

To the right of the time period drop-down menu, there is a download
button, which allows the user to download the data presented on the
`Retention Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} page as an Excel
file.

### MRR breakdown

To access the `MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
reporting page, navigate to `Subscriptions
app --> Reporting --> MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}.

By default, the data displayed on the `MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} reporting page is in graph view, with the
`Bar Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option and
`Stacked`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option selected.

A default filter is also available in the search bar for
`Event Date: Month > Event Type`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

{.align-center}

When the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on
the `MRR Breakdown`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page is
clicked, a series of metric-related options become available.

{.align-center}

The metric-related options in the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on the `MRR
Breakdown`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page are:

-   `Active Subscriptions Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `ARR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `MRR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Count`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

The `MRR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} measure option is
selected by default.
:::

::: {.tip}
::: {.title}
Tip
:::

To make a different measure option the default, first, select the
desired measure from the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
drop-down menu. Then, click the `fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text
role="icon"} `(down
arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon in the search bar to
open the mega menu of filters and groupings.

In the `Favorites`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} column, click the
`fa-caret-down`{.interpreted-text role="icon"}
`(down arrow)`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} icon beside
`Save current search`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} to reveal a
field, where a title can be entered, along with two
checkboxes:`Default filter`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} and
`Shared`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

Tick the checkbox for `Default filter`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"}, and click `Save`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

That newly-chosen measure option is now the default option that appears
when this reporting page is accessed.
:::

### MRR analysis

To access the `MRR Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
reporting page, navigate to `Subscriptions
app --> Reporting --> MRR Timeline`{.interpreted-text
role="menuselection"}.

By default, the data displayed on the `MRR Analysis`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} reporting page is in graph view, with the
`Line Chart`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option,
`Stacked`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option, and
`Cumulative`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} option selected.

A default filter is also found in the search bar for
`Event Date: Month`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}.

{.align-center}

When the `Measures`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on
the `MRR Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page is
clicked, a series of metric-related options become available.

{.align-center}

The metric-related options in the `Measures`{.interpreted-text
role="guilabel"} drop-down menu on the `MRR
Analysis`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} reporting page are:

-   `Active Subscriptions Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `ARR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `MRR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}
-   `Count`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"}

::: {.note}
::: {.title}
Note
:::

The `MRR Change`{.interpreted-text role="guilabel"} measure option is
selected by default.
:::
